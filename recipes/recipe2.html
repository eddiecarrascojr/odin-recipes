Ukrainian Dumplings

Family recipe for Traditional Ukrainian Dumplings. They are also known as veronicas in Ukrainian.

Ingredients

  • all natural flour
  • water
  • potatoes
  • onions
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil
  • meat of your choice usually ground beef mixed with ground pork is recommended.

Cooking Instructions

  • Peel and boil the potatoes until they are soft.
  • Drain the potatoes and mash them with a fork or potato masher.
  • Season the potatoes with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Set the potatoes aside to cool.
  • While the potatoes are cooling, prepare the meat filling.
  • Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  • Add the onions and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  • Add the ground meat and cook until it is browned and cooked through.
  • Season the meat with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Set the meat aside to cool.
  • Once the potatoes and meat are cool, you can start assembling the dumplings.
  • Roll out the dough on a floured surface until it is about 1/8 inch thick.
  • Make small balls of the meat and potatoes.
  • Place the balls in the center of the dough circles.
  • Bring the edges of the dough together and pinch them to seal the dumplings.
  • Boil the dumplings in a large pot of salted water until they float to the surface.
  • Remove the dumplings from the water after 15 minutes and serve hot with sour cream or butter.
  • Enjoy!
