Best Protein Shake ever

Personal recipe for the best protein shake ever. It is a great way to start your day or to refuel after a workout.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • 1 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup of ice

    • How to make the Protein Shake. Hopefully you have a blender.

  • Place all the ingredients in a blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Pour into a glass and enjoy!
