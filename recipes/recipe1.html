Recipe for Mexican Hot Choco

This is a family recipe for champurado also known as Mexican hot chocolate. This served in cold weather when family is visiting for the holidays.

Ingredients you will need.

  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of masa harina
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract

Optional Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of evaporated milk

These two are removed for the non-dairy folks. If you do include them in the recipe it makes it so much better.

Cooking Instructions

  • Add the water and stir pot in low heat.
  • Optionally you can also add the milk and stir until well combined.
  • Add the water, brown sugar, cocoa powder, masa harina, cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
  • Stir until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  • Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Remove from heat and let cool slightly before serving.
  • Serve hot with a sprinkle of cinnamon on top.
  • Enjoy!
